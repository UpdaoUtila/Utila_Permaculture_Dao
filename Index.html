About Utila Permaculture DAO

Welcome to the Utila Permaculture DAO project! We aim to create a sustainable and self-sufficient ecosystem in Utila through permaculture principles and decentralized collaboration. Join us in making a positive impact on Utila and the environment.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) that drives sustainable development projects in Utila. Through community empowerment and permaculture practices, we strive to create harmony between humans, nature, and the environment.

Contribute to the Project

We welcome contributions from developers, designers, content creators, permaculture experts, and anyone passionate about our mission. To contribute, fork the repository on GitHub and submit pull requests with your changes.
